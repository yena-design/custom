How It Works

A simple process from thrift find to custom fit.

1. Find Your Piece

Shop your favorite thrift store and choose the item you want customized.

2. Scan or Book

Use the QR code or website to request service.

3. Consult With Yena

Share your fit goals, measurements, or redesign idea.

4. Customize

Yena alters, repairs, reshapes, or redesigns the piece.

5. Pick Up & Wear

Your thrift find becomes a custom piece you love.
